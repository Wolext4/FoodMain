Move More

Here are some simple activities to increase your physical activity level.

1. Gardening

At-home gardening is a great way to increase your physical activity.

2. Sweeping

Household chores like sweeping help keep you active.

3. Driving

Driving yourself instead of using public transport can increase movement.

4. Jogging

Jogging is a simple way to boost physical activity and stay fit.

5. Strolling

Daily walks in the neighborhood, parks, or markets can help improve activity levels.

6. Washing Plates

Simple activities like washing dishes contribute to movement.

7. Cycling

Cycling is an excellent way to stay physically active and improve endurance.

8. Swimming

Swimming works out the whole body and increases activity levels significantly.
